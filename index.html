Does your business meet the following criteria:


  • Your business is either:
    • Registered under the Companies Act. or;
    • Registered under the Limited Partnerships Act. or;
    • A Māori incorporation or trust established under the Te Ture Whenua Māori Act, a trust set up to manage assets as part of a Treaty of Waitangi settlement, a Māori statutory body, or a business controlled by one or more of these entities.
  • Your business is not state owned.
  • The registered address for your business is located in New Zealand.
  • At least one of the directors/partners of the business is a New Zealand tax resident.
  • Your business is currently, or planning to spend money on research and development.
